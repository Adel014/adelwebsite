How I Celebrated My Last Birthday Party

I celebrated my last birthday party on the 14th of April, 2024. It was an unforgettable experience as I celebrated it with family and friends. There was lots of foods to eat, lots of games to play and most especially, lots of places to go!

Family Time

I woke up in the morning to find all my family members like my grandparents, siblings, aunts and my uncles, seated waiting for me in the sitting room. It was a splendid surprise.

The Birthday Song

Another part of my birthday that kept me smiling all day was the birthday song. Each time they sang it, made a wide smile unwind on my face.

Food Galore

If I wanted to write the amount of food that was on my plate that day, you wouldn't believe me! My dish was filled with scrumptious delicacies that had a golden aroma. I can say that you would be able to smell the food from three blocks away!

B-Day Outing

On that day, we roamed through the streets of Benin-City, stopping at one point to the other. We had ice-cream, pizza, pastries and we went shopping!

Overall, my experience was blissful and it had a long-lasting memory that I will never forget. I can't wait for my next birthday!
